Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mtn Mobile Money,Master card,Telecel Cash,Visa Cards and Airtel-tigo cash

How do i order and pay for my food?

Place order and chose mode of payment through the various payment methods made available to you.

When will my payment be processed?

Payments are typically processed immediately,but may take up to a few minutes to complete in certain cases.

How it works

What happens after i place payment?

After placing payment you'd receive a confirmation email to confirm your payment.

How do you ensure my payment method is secure?

We use industry-level -SSL encryption and follow best practices to ensure your payment information is secure.

How do i request a refund or cancellation

To request a refund or cancellation please visit our contact us page and place a call to communicate with our support team.

Delivery delay

How do i track my order?

You can track the location of your delivery by using the token number given to you in your payment confirmation email.

Why is my order delaying?

We apologise for the delay,possible reasons for delay may be from vendor's speed or rate of delivery of service.

Will i be notified about any issues with my deliveries?

We'll notify you via your email or mobile phone number.

Preorder

Can i pre-order meals before the shop opens?

Yes,but within periods us such you would be notified to avail space for customer's with current need of the food items.

No,all others are given equal treatments to ensure fairness in service.
